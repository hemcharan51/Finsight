# Acme Corp — Form 10-K, Fiscal Year 2023

Item 1. Business

Acme Corp designs and manufactures industrial automation equipment for global
manufacturing customers. Fiscal year 2023 ended December 31, 2023.

Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis (MD&A)

Revenue grew steadily over the three-year period, rising from $29,000 million in
fiscal 2021 to $33,900 million in fiscal 2023, a compound annual growth rate of
+8.1% CAGR. Management attributes the trend to share gains in robotics and a full
order book entering 2024. The outlook for fiscal 2024 is for continued
mid-single-digit revenue growth and stable margins.

Key risks include exposure to cyclical capital-equipment spending, supply-chain
concentration in a small number of component vendors, and foreign-exchange
volatility across European and Asian markets.

Item 8. Financial Statements

Consolidated Statements of Operations (in millions, fiscal year 2023).
For fiscal year 2023, total revenue was $33,900 million, up from the prior year.
Gross profit was $13,560 million. Operating income reached $5,400 million. Net
income for the year totaled $4,200 million.

Consolidated Balance Sheet (in millions, as of December 31, 2023).
Total assets stood at $52,000 million. Total current assets were $18,000 million.
Total current liabilities were $9,000 million. Total debt was $12,000 million.
Total shareholders' equity was $24,000 million. Cash and cash equivalents were
$6,000 million.

Research and development expense was $2,400 million for the fiscal year.
