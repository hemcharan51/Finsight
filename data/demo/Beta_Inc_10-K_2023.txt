# Beta Inc — Form 10-K, Fiscal Year 2023

Item 1. Business

Beta Inc is a diversified consumer-products company operating across home care,
personal care, and packaged foods. Fiscal year 2023 ended December 31, 2023.

Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis (MD&A)

Over the trailing three years revenue increased from $19,800 million in fiscal
2021 to $21,200 million in fiscal 2023, a compound annual growth rate of +3.4%
CAGR. Growth was driven by pricing actions that offset soft volumes. The outlook
for fiscal 2024 anticipates low-single-digit revenue growth as volume recovers.

Key risks include commodity-input inflation, private-label competition pressuring
shelf share, and customer concentration among large retailers.

Item 8. Financial Statements

Consolidated Statements of Operations (in millions, fiscal year 2023).
For fiscal year 2023, total revenue was $21,200 million. Gross profit was $7,420
million. Operating income reached $2,650 million. Net income for the year totaled
$1,910 million.

Consolidated Balance Sheet (in millions, as of December 31, 2023).
Total assets stood at $31,000 million. Total current assets were $9,500 million.
Total current liabilities were $6,200 million. Total debt was $9,800 million.
Total shareholders' equity was $14,000 million. Cash and cash equivalents were
$3,100 million.

Research and development expense was $1,500 million for the fiscal year.
