# Gamma Ltd — Annual Report 2023

Business Review

Gamma Ltd is a regional logistics and warehousing operator. The financial year
2023 ended December 31, 2023. This annual report follows a condensed disclosure
format and does not present a separate consolidated bottom-line earnings line in
the summary financial review section reproduced here.

Operating and Financial Review

Revenue rose from $7,600 million in 2021 to $8,400 million in 2023, a compound
annual growth rate of +5.0% CAGR, supported by new distribution contracts. The
outlook for 2024 is cautious given fuel-cost volatility.

Key risks include fuel-price exposure, labor availability in distribution centers,
and customer contract renewal risk.

Summary Financials (in millions, financial year 2023).
For the financial year 2023, total revenue was $8,400 million. Gross profit was
$2,600 million. Operating income reached $700 million.

Balance Sheet Summary (in millions, as of December 31, 2023).
Total assets stood at $12,000 million. Total current assets were $4,000 million.
Total current liabilities were $3,500 million. Total debt was $5,200 million.
Total shareholders' equity was $4,800 million. Cash and cash equivalents were
$900 million.
