# Nimbus Systems — Form 10-K, Fiscal Year 2024

Item 1. Business

Nimbus Systems provides cloud-native data infrastructure and analytics software to
enterprise customers on a subscription basis. Fiscal year 2024 ended January 31, 2025.

Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis (MD&A)

Revenue grew rapidly over the three-year period, rising from $8,500 million in
fiscal 2022 to $12,000 million in fiscal 2024, a compound annual growth rate of
+18.8% CAGR. Management attributes the trend to strong net revenue retention and
expansion into international markets. The outlook for fiscal 2025 is for continued
high-teens revenue growth as the company scales its platform business.

Key risks include intense competition from larger cloud platform vendors,
dependence on a concentrated set of hyperscale hosting providers, and the cost of
sustaining elevated research and development investment.

Item 8. Financial Statements

Consolidated Statements of Operations (in millions, fiscal year 2024).
For fiscal year 2024, total revenue was $12,000 million, up sharply from the prior
year. Gross profit was $9,000 million. Operating income reached $2,400 million. Net
income for the year totaled $1,800 million.

Consolidated Balance Sheet (in millions, as of January 31, 2025).
Total assets stood at $20,000 million. Total current assets were $8,000 million.
Total current liabilities were $4,000 million. Total debt was $3,000 million.
Total shareholders' equity was $12,000 million. Cash and cash equivalents were
$5,000 million.

Research and development expense was $3,600 million for the fiscal year.
