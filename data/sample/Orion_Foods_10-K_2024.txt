# Orion Foods — Form 10-K, Fiscal Year 2024

Item 1. Business

Orion Foods is a diversified packaged-foods manufacturer selling branded grocery
products across North America and Europe. Fiscal year 2024 ended December 31, 2024.

Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis (MD&A)

Revenue rose modestly over the three-year period, from $42,000 million in fiscal
2022 to $45,000 million in fiscal 2024, a compound annual growth rate of +3.5%
CAGR. Growth was driven by pricing actions that offset soft volumes in the
center-of-store categories. The outlook for fiscal 2025 is for low-single-digit
revenue growth with a gradual recovery in volume.

Key risks include input-cost inflation in commodities and packaging, customer
concentration among a small number of large retailers, and shifting consumer
preferences toward private-label and fresh products.

Item 8. Financial Statements

Consolidated Statements of Operations (in millions, fiscal year 2024).
For fiscal year 2024, total revenue was $45,000 million. Gross profit was $13,500
million. Operating income reached $4,500 million. Net income for the year totaled
$2,700 million.

Consolidated Balance Sheet (in millions, as of December 31, 2024).
Total assets stood at $60,000 million. Total current assets were $15,000 million.
Total current liabilities were $12,000 million. Total debt was $25,000 million.
Total shareholders' equity was $20,000 million. Cash and cash equivalents were
$2,000 million.

Research and development expense was $600 million for the fiscal year.
